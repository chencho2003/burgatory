Rule And Regulations

            The Company Rules and Regulations for the Employees
            The following rules and regulations shall impose on all employees of the company. Some
            rules and regulations include:
            1. Every employee of the Burgatory company must have to follow and obey the
            act, guidance, policy, rules, and regulations imposed by the company authority and
            applicable from time to time.
            2. The company expects formal and acceptable behaviors from the employees of the
            Burgatory. The prime motive of the Burgatory company is to attract
            customers through outstanding conduct of the employees.
            3. Employees are liable to protect the company's belongings what they are using for office
            purposes. They will be responsible for breaking any type of official equipment.
            4. Further, our company the Burgatory very aware of maintaining the record,
            therefore, every employee has to be conscious to protect documents. The office will not
            accept any argument without proper evidence.
